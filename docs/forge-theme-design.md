### Typography

**Headings**: Bold, strong fonts that feel like hammered metal
- Primary: 'Cinzel', serif (medieval/blacksmith feel)
- Alternative: 'Bebas Neue', sans-serif (strong, industrial)

**Body**: Readable but with character
- Primary: 'Roboto Slab', serif (sturdy, readable)
- Code: 'Fira Code', monospace (technical precision)

### Visual Elements

**Textures**
- Hammered metal texture for cards/panels
- Rough stone texture for backgrounds
- Wood grain for navigation elements
- Glowing ember particles for active states

**Borders & Edges**
- Rough, hand-forged edges (not perfectly rounded)
- Rivets and bolts as decorative elements
- Glowing borders for active/focused elements

**Shadows**
- Deep, dramatic shadows (forge lighting)
- Glow effects for hot/active elements
- Ember particle effects on hover
